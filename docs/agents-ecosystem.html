Skills vs Agents — What's the Difference
CapabilitySkills (slash commands)Agents (autonomous workflows)
You give it data✓ You paste it in✓ It fetches data itself
Searches SharePoint✓ On your command✓ Automatically, multi-source
Multi-step reasoningSingle pass✓ Iterates, self-corrects
Generates files✓ With export step✓ Automatically creates PPTX, HTML, docs
Runs on a scheduleManual only✓ Can run weekly/daily automatically
Chains tools togetherOne tool at a time✓ Search → Analyse → Generate → Export → Notify
Human input neededYes — you drive itMinimal — just the trigger
Best forStructured, repeatable tasksComplex workflows that span hours of manual work

The 6 Agents We're Building
🎨

/deck-builder

Presentation Factory

  • Any topic → full branded PPTX in seconds
  • All Hands, Sales Decks, Exec Briefings, Roadshows, Partner Pitches
  • Pulls real content from SharePoint automatically
  • Consistent UiPath styling, no blank slide anxiety
Saves 3–4h / deck High Priority
🎙️

/meeting-intel

Meeting Intelligence Agent

  • Paste transcript or rough notes → full structured output
  • Extracts: decisions, action items, risks, open questions
  • Generates next meeting agenda automatically
  • Saves output back to SharePoint
Saves 30–60min / meeting High Priority
💡

/idea-machine

Idea & Business Case Generator

  • Give it a problem or challenge → 10 ranked ideas with rationale
  • Generates full business case for each idea
  • Checks SharePoint for existing work on the topic
  • Outputs as slides, doc, or email
Saves 2h / ideation session Medium Priority
🔄

/content-remix

Content Repurposing Agent

  • One doc → 6 formats instantly
  • Exec summary, email, FAQ, slides, LinkedIn post, talking points
  • Point it at any SharePoint doc
  • Adapts tone per audience (partner, CFO, PSM, customer)
Saves 1–2h / content piece Medium Priority
📊

/partner-pulse

Partner Health Monitor

  • Scans all partner data → flags at-risk partners
  • RAG health score for every partner automatically
  • Suggests intervention actions per partner
  • Weekly digest delivered to your inbox
Saves 3h / week monitoring Medium Priority
🚨

/escalation-handler

Escalation Triage Agent

  • Paste escalation details → instant structured response plan
  • Classifies severity, finds similar past cases in SharePoint
  • Generates stakeholder comms, RACI, timeline
  • Drafts partner-facing and internal messages
Saves 1h / escalation Lower Priority
